### **A Travel Broad**

# Wander with Kids. Plan Like a Pro.

Fun, fully researched family travel.

Hi, I’m Nicole! I’m a Southern California-based mom to two energetic kids (6 and 4), married to an incredible husband, and always planning our next trip. Traveling has long been a passion of mine, and now I get to experience it through family adventures–making memories, exploring new places, and occasionally navigating meltdowns in unexpected settings. 

What I love almost as much as traveling is the planning. I thrive on researching destinations, uncovering hidden gems, and building detailed itineraries that balance fun, efficiency, and real-life family logistics. I know not everyone enjoys spreadsheets and Google Maps the way I do, so this blog is my way of sharing the work behind the trips–both the research and what actually worked for us.
Our travel style reflects who we are as a family. You’ll find lots of outdoor adventures, kid-approved stops, breweries (we love good beer), sporting events, and local experiences. We tend to skip big tourist attractions and traditional museums unless they are interactive, and we gravitate toward smaller cities with a strong local vibe. Fair warning: my kids are very much “go-go-go,” so these itineraries pack a lot in. Take what works, skip what doesn’t, and make it your own. 

I hope this blog helps you plan trips that feel doable, memorable and fun. If you’d like help building a custom itinerary for your family, feel free to reach out at [email]. I’d be happy to help.
